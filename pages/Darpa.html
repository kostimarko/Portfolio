Darpa Dun Goofed

A collection of projects exposing the past, present and future of the internet, using three separate mediums for the content. The title is generated from Darpa who had ultimate control of the internet and then released it to the world only to want to control it again.

The Past is represented in the form of print, the project is named Darpa Dun Goofed takes a look at the future of the internet. The content is derived from the importance of anonymity.

The Present is represented in the form of a tumblr blog named how to break google, in which I take a look at the words that turn up no search results in googles all powerful search engine.

The Future is represented in the form of an ePub, whose content consists of the history of where the internet came from.

The Poster

A marketing poster used to educate the viewer on the three moving peices of the project. Using a modified javascript function that is meant to open windows to the each project, I edited various parts of the code to enable the user to read what each project is based in.
