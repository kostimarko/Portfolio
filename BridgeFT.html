Intro

Bridge Portfolio was acquired by a group of friends with the intention of creating a SaaS platform for financial advisors. They wanted to bridge the gap between ever evolving technology and financial advisors. Creating more time for these advisors to spend with their clients, rather than spending hours on end doing operational work.

Research

I first started doing some research in competitors and other companies in this space.

Interviews

I interviewed all of the co-founders to get a sense of why they were interested in creating this company. They all said that they saw an opening in the market where advisors under a certain level of Assets Under Management did not have a software solution that fit their needs, there was no room for growth at some of these Advisor firms with the amount they were paying for software or other employees to do the operational work. Their solution was building technology around these advisors, helping them growth their business at a much faster pace. They wanted to give an advisor a command line to their financial data.

Brand

The Command Line, the piece of technology on every computer that gives the user root access to anything and everything on your operating system.
