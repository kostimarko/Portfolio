Circle Reading Room

A proposed Environmental design for the Richard Daley library using historical images from The University of Illinois at Chicago image archive.

The past represents where we have come from, how far we have come, and how far we can go. This space is a quiet space, where students can study, can self reflect on what they have learned at UIC, and where they will go after UIC.
